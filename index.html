Riccardo "Blanco" Fabbriconi

The singer that helped me overcome apathy

Riccardo Fabbriconi is an italian singer going by the pen name of "Blanco", he is famous for his dynamic and energetic style.

He debuts in 2020 with 3 songs by the Italian producer "Michelangelo", with which he still holds numerous featurings, and soon gets the attention of Universal Music. By 2023, he publishes 2 albums and holds a stable position in the national charts.

Blanco is capable of singing in a wide range, creating pop and rock songs that are brimming with emotions and can convey their own meaning in an incredibly direct way, despite being only 20
